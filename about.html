About Me:


Hi, thanks for stopping by! My name is Diana & I'm an adventurous
and energetic pet lover and caregiver located in San Diego.

As a pet mom myself, I know how important it is to find quality
care & the right fit for your fur baby. I also know how expensive
pet care can be. I want to take the stress out of pet-sitting, and
make it fun and affordable for you and your pet!

I am licensed and insured for your peace of mind, and am experienced
with senior pets & can administer pet medications if needed as well!

Feel free to reach out to me and let's discuss your pet's needs!
I can't wait to become their new best friend and learn all of their
adorable and funny quirks!

Happy tails,
Diana :)
